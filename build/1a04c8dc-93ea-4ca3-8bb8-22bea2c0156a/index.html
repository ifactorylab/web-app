West Driving Academy

Welcome to West Driving Academy where you can arrange your first driving lesson with confidence.

We offer driving lessons in West London including Ealing, Acton, Chiswick, Brentford, Kew, Chelsea, Kensington, Hammersmith, Richmond, Hounslow, Heston, Isleworth, Hanwell, Uxbridge, Northolt, Southall and Greenford through to Slough, Windsor, Eaton, Langley, Cippenham and surronding areas.

Pass Plus

A Pass Plus course is designed to improve driver skills and safety. It can be taken at anytime although it is most useful for new drivers who have passed within the last year. Our instructors are Pass Plus registered (ADI).
